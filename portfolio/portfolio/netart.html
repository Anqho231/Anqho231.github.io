The Jam Game

My Net Art activity is the Jam Game, where you, the main character, suddenly notice falling strawberries coming from the sky. Your job is to choose the right options or dialogues, and if you win, you get to create a perfect jam in the game. I chose my topic to be Jams on my GitHub website because I grew up loving jam throughout my childhood, even used it as a dipping on my favorite foods, chicken nuggets.
