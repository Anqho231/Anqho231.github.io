P5.js Beach Game

The Beach Game allows players to interact around a beach and catch what pops out, such as Batman surfing. Every time I always go to the beach, I always encounter interesting things including people wearing costumes. It symbolizes what makes the beach very unusual, yet memorable for goers to cherish.

Finding Schneider & Arcana: A Reverse 1999 Fan-Game

I also created a catching game, Reverse, based on a new video game that I am currently addicted to on my phone called Reverse 1999, a Chinese European steampunk story based role-playing gacha game. In this game, players become Vertin, the main character with the top hat, and explore around the jazz house to look for the Manus Vindicatae, a secret terrorist organization. Meanwhile, you came across members including Schneider and Arcana, and the goal is to catch them before the world goes back in time from the storm. It's a fun and easy way to introduce the audience to Reverse 1999 and its world. I highly recommend to those who are familiar with gacha games like Genshin Impact, or turn-based playing games like Arknights or SMT: Persona.
